Tropeiro Beans

Description

Tropeiro beans is a traditional and flavorful dish from Brazilian cuisine. Made with ingredients such as bacon, calabrese sausage, olive oil, onion, garlic, cooked beans, bacon broth, eggs, parsley, cassava flour, salt, and pepper, this dish offers a unique combination of flavors.

This dish is a true blend of textures and aromas, making it a delicious side dish for events and barbecues. Prepare this tropeiro beans recipe to enjoy the authentic taste of Brazilian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 150 g of diced bacon
  • 1 coffee cup of olive oil
  • 6 mashed garlic cloves
  • 1 bacon broth cube
  • Parsley to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • 250 g of diced calabrese sausage
  • 2 diced onions
  • 500 g of cooked and drained beans
  • 4 fried eggs, whole and chopped
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of raw cassava flour
  • Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Fry the bacon and sausage separately until golden brown.
  2. In the same pan, saut√© the onion and garlic.
  3. Add the drained beans and bacon broth to the pan.
  4. Mix in the bacon, sausage, cassava flour, and chopped fried eggs.
  5. Generously add parsley to enhance the flavors.
  6. Adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
